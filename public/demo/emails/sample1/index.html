ONLINE MASTER OF MUSIC IN MUSIC EDUCATION
Your performance as a music educator inspires generations to appreciate the arts. A graduate degree from Kent State University can help you make an even greater impact on your students' lives. Learn how to strike a chord that resonates.

We share your passion for music education. That's why our Master of Music in music education online program is designed to help music educators like you amplify your influence.
Our online Master of Music in music education program helps you:
• Inspire change with a practice-oriented curriculum immediately applicable in the classroom.
• Individualize learning and use technology to connect with students.
• Pursue opportunities with an expanded skill set and experience in new music areas.
Ready to be the muse in your classroom?
CREDIBLE
Choose a program regionally accredited and nationally recognized by NASM and NCATE.
APPLIED
Apply new tools and strategies to impact your classroom and district.
 
VERSATILE
Broaden your career and knowledge with a focus in two of three areas: general, instrumental or choral.
