# Brett Snyder Portfolio — Northstar

## Mission
A professional portfolio and resume site for Brett Snyder. Showcases work history, projects, and skills to potential employers and clients. Fast, clean, and easy to keep current.

## Audience
Hiring managers, recruiters, and potential clients evaluating Brett as a candidate or freelancer. They need to quickly understand who he is, what he's built, and how to contact him.

## Success
A visitor can find Brett's work, read his background, download his resume, and reach out — all within a minute. Content stays current with minimal effort.

## Non-Negotiables
1. Performance first — fast load times, optimised images, no unnecessary dependencies
2. Content is managed via Markdown files — no CMS, no database
3. Resume PDF is always in sync with the HTML source — generated via `npm run generate-resume`
4. No backend, no auth, no database — static-first

## Brand & Tone
Professional but personal. Approachable, not corporate. Shows real work, not marketing copy.
